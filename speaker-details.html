Speaker Details

Kacem Zoughari

Kacem Zoughari

Dr. Kacem Zoughari is a well known Japanese History and Martial Arts researcher and practitioner. Born in Paris, France he began studying Martial Arts as a teenager and quickly began visiting Japan to further his studies. He has practiced Ninjutsu for well over 34 years and is a personal student of Hatsumi Masaaki Soke and Ishizuka Tetsuji Soke.
