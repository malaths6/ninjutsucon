About The Event

About The Event

Seminar theme:

  • Bōjutsu (棒術)
  • Kenjutsu (剣術)
  • hanbō (半棒)
  • Shinken Gata
Presenting to you a Bujinkan Ninpo (武神館) convention with Dr. Kacem Zoughari.

We would like to extend this invitation to martial arts practitioners of all styles and ranks.

Bujinkan beginners and practitioners of other martial arts can benefit from this seminar alike. It is a great opportunity to gain insights not only into Bujinkan Ninpo Taijutsu, but also Japanese armed combat, language, history and culture.
