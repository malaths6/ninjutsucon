About The Event

Seminar theme:

  • Bōjutsu (棒術)
  • Kenjutsu (剣術)
  • hanbō (半棒)

Presenting to you a Ninjutsu (忍術) convention with Dr. Kacem Zoughari.

We would like to extend this invitation to martial arts practitioners of all styles and ranks.

Ninjutsu beginners and practitioners of other martial arts can benefit from this seminar alike. It is a great opportunity to gain insights not only into Ninjutsu, but also Japanese armed combat, language, history and culture.

F.A.Q

  • Which airport is closest to the event?

    Larnaca airport is the closest airport to the event. It's a 35 minute drive to the hotel where the event is taking place.

  • How do I get to the event from Larnaca airport?

    To travel using public transport buses from Larnaca airport to Ayia Napa involves catching a bus from Larnaca airport to Larnaca city and then changing at Finikoudes bus stop for a bus to Ayia Napa.

    There is no direct public transport bus from Larnaca airport to Ayia Napa. There are frequent bus services to use during the day but please be aware that services are less frequent in the evening and no services will be available during night hours.

  • How do I pay for the event?

    The hotel expenses should be paid directly to the hotel - please state that you will attend the event in order to get the special price.

    The money for the training needs to be paid directly to Lyndsey.
